Dreams of Darkness

Haunted House

The Dreams of Darkness is Redding's only professional haunted house and one of the longest haunted attractions in America.
At nearly 40 minutes, Dreams of Darkness is one of the longest haunted attractions in America and the only professional haunted house in Redding. You will walk an unguided path from the ghost towns and mines of the Wild West, to the bayous and swamps of the Deep South, through the home of The Clown, and through our newly-renovated Hellevator, encountering an unknowable terror at every turn.
